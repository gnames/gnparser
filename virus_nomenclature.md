# ICVCN rules

## Rules

### Genus and Higher Ranks

For ranks from Genus to Realm, taxon names:

* Must have mandatory suffix endings (see tables below)
* Must be capitalized
* Must be italicized
* Cannot contain hyphens
* Must contain only base Latin characters.

### Species Names

Species names consist of exactly TWO components separated by a space:

* First component: genus name (capitalized, e.g., "Triavirus")
* Second component: species epithet

The species epithet may be:

* Traditional lowercase (e.g., "encephalitidis" in "Orthobunyavirus encephalitidis")
* Mixed case with numbers (e.g., "phi2958PVL" in "Triavirus phi2958PVL")

Character rules for species epithets:

* Hyphens ('-') are allowed
* Numbers are allowed
* Hyphens cannot separate numbers from letters
  (numbers are not separated by '-' from the rest of the epithet)
* Only base Latin characters, hyphens and numbers are allowed.
